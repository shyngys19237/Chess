## Product Description

MateMind is a Chess.com-inspired chess platform for playing, reviewing, and improving at chess through AI-powered analysis.

The app allows users to play against Stockfish-powered chess bots, review completed games, see move classifications, analyze accuracy, and understand key mistakes through a coach-style Game Review interface. MateMind also includes social and retention features such as friend games by invite link, profiles, virtual coins, cosmetic chess piece skins, and leaderboard architecture.

## Who It Is For

MateMind is designed for casual and improving chess players who want more than just a chess board. It helps users understand why they won or lost, what mistakes they made, and what they should improve next.

## Why It Is Valuable

Most beginner and intermediate chess players struggle to learn from their games. MateMind turns every match into an interactive learning experience by combining gameplay, engine-based analysis, and simple explanations.

The project also demonstrates startup and product thinking: it includes retention systems such as profiles, coins, skins, and future monetization ideas like premium customization and Pro features.

## Key Features

- Play against Stockfish-powered bots
- Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced bot levels
- Chess.com-inspired Game Review
- Move classifications such as Best, Excellent, Mistake, and Blunder
- Accuracy scores for both players
- Evaluation graph
- Best move recommendations
- Friend match by invite link
- Profile and avatar system architecture
- Virtual coins and match rewards
- Cosmetic chess piece skin shop
- Leaderboard architecture
- Supabase-ready authentication and database schema
- Mobile-responsive interface

MateMind is not just a chess demo. It is designed as a startup-style prototype with:
